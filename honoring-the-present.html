Honoring the Present

Make Mobile a sports & entertainment destination

Sports is a growth industry and, as a Foundation, we can play an important role in supporting every effort to make Mobile a better place to live, work and play. Ultimately, we want Mobile to become THE destination city for sports and entertainment in the Southeast.

The Mobile Sports Hall of Fame Foundation is a community-wide organization benefiting all citizens through its connection to the sports world. The Foundation will work diligently with City and County officials, local and international businesses, public and private schools, essential service organizations, existing cultural assets and other entities to make athletics accessible to all and move Mobile forward.

With an emphasis on serving our community’s youth through education, participation in sports, health & wellness and leadership training, the Foundation can play an important role in developing the next generation to lead successful lives.
